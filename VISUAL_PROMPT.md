## 🎯 메인 프롬프트 (영어, Midjourney 최적화)

```
Split-screen 16:9 isometric illustration of a Korean industrial complex,
left half grayscale and desolate at dusk (gray factories, empty parking lot,
a lonely young man walking alone with a phone), right half vibrant RPG
theme-park version of the SAME location at golden hour — colorful quest
pins hovering above buildings (running track, social dining kitchen, book
club library, climbing gym, plogging zone, coffee shop), floating coin
icons, glowing XP particles, young people in small tribes playing
together, hot air balloon overhead, cheerful crowd, neon outlines, game
map UI overlay with level bar and coin counter in the corner.

Style: isometric, playful but sophisticated, pastel + vibrant accents
(emerald #22C55E, purple #A855F7, gold #FACC15, soft coral),
Studio Ghibli meets Pokémon GO UI, clean vector lines, soft shadows,
cinematic lighting, high detail.

Aspect ratio: 16:9, ultra wide, product key visual quality.

--ar 16:9 --style raw --stylize 400 --v 6.1
```

스타일: 아이소메트릭, 따뜻하면서 세련된, 스튜디오 지브리 × 포켓몬GO UI,

## 💡 프롬프트 사용 팁

1. **Midjourney:** 메인 프롬프트 그대로 복사 → `/imagine` 뒤에 붙여넣기
2. **DALL·E 3 (ChatGPT):** 한국어 버전 복사 → "16:9로 만들어줘" 추가
3. **Ideogram:** 단순 버전 추천, 텍스트 오버레이까지 넣고 싶으면 프롬프트에 `"SANDAN PARK"` 텍스트 포함
4. **Gemini 2.5 Image / Nano Banana:** 한국어 버전이 더 잘 이해됨
